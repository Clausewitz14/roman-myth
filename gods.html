Important Gods in Roman Mythology

Scroll down to see the 3 most important gods in Roman mythology!

Jupiter

Jupiter was the most loved god in Roman mythology. His brothers, Hades and Neptune, were overshadowed by their brother's importance. Jupiter was the King of Gods and the god of the skies and lightning.

Juno

Juno was the wife of Jupiter and the Roman goddess of females. Here religious symbols were the peacock, cattle and the lion. She was the goddess of marriage and when angered, became extremely powerful.

Minerva

Minerva was the Roman goddess of wisdom. She was born out of Jupiter's brain, so she has no mother. Her symbol was the owl, which symbolized her connection with knowledge.
