Roman Mythology - Crash Course


Roman mythology was derived from Greek Mythology. The Romans took Greek gods and stories , and edited them to make them 'Romanish'. They changed the gods' name and stories. Zeus became Jupiter, Hera became Juno and so on. This caused a rift between Romans and Greeks. They fought lots of wars and each side trained spies to listen what the other side was saying.
